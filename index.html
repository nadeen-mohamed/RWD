One of the first steps in hiring any talent is to determine which skills you need for your project. You know you need help with writing and translation, but what specific things are you trying to do? Upwork ...

A writing and translation project includes the following:
  • Deliverables: What do you need done? Perhaps you need a professional to write blog posts, ad copy, or translate documents.
  • Skills: Identify the writing and translation talent needed to complete the project (e.g., copywriter or French-to-English translator).
  • Project Length: Your job post should indicate whether this is a smaller or larger project.
  • Budget: Set a budget and note your preference for either an hourly rate or fixed-price contract.
